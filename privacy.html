Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 6, 2025

Summary: Chito collects minimal data to provide personalized news briefings. We don't sell your data. We don't track you across other apps or websites.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your phone number or email address for authentication.
  • Preferences: Topics you select, language preference, and notification settings.
  • Constituency Selection: If you choose your constituency for election coverage.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Token: For sending push notifications (briefing reminders, breaking news).
  • Reading Activity: Which briefings you complete and articles you read, to track your streak and personalize content.
  • App Usage: Basic analytics to understand how features are used and improve the app.

Information We Don't Collect

  • Precise location data
  • Contacts or address book
  • Photos or files
  • Browsing history outside the app

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Deliver personalized news briefings based on your selected topics
  • Send push notifications (morning/evening briefings, breaking news, streak reminders)
  • Track your reading streak and activity statistics
  • Show election candidates and results for your constituency
  • Improve the app based on usage patterns

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: We use Supabase (database), Firebase (notifications), and Google Cloud (hosting) to operate the app. These providers process data on our behalf under strict agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect rights and safety.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Your data is stored securely on Supabase servers.
  • We use encryption for data in transit (HTTPS) and at rest.
  • Reading statistics and preferences are also stored locally on your device.

5. Your Choices

  • Notifications: You can disable notifications in app settings or your device settings.
  • Topics: You can change your selected topics anytime in the app.
  • Account Deletion: Contact us at privacy@chito.app to request account deletion.

6. Children's Privacy

Chito is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes through the app or by updating the "Last updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data:

  • Email: privacy@chito.app
